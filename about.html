Visson and Mission

It is a Discussion Forum created for educational purposes.
It achieves and supports students and teachers by facilitating reflection, questioning by self and others,
collaboration and by providing context for engaging in higher order thinking.


Why to Use?

*Promotes autonomous learning by providing opportunities
for students to take more control of their learning.
*Motivates students to become better readers and writers.
*Promotes discussion among students and teachers via this.
